Zodiac Signs

Amoung your house and planet placements, you will find a zodiac sign that rules that placement. Meaning it is what gives it a unique characteristic. There are 12 signs in total separated into 4 elements, fire, water, air, and earth.

  • Fire Signs: Aries, Leo, Saggitarius
  • Water Signs: Cancer, Pisces, Scorpio
  • Air Signs: Aquarius, Gemini, Libra
  • Earth Signs: Taurus, Capricorn, Virgo

Planets Placements

Planet placements, can tell us where we store our energy. It can also tell us what our weaknesses and strengths are. In total there are 10 planets, Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto. The most important ones being the Sun and Moon placements.

Sun Sign

The sun placements represents your core personality. It is the exterior part of you that everyone sees. This is the placement that most people know. When wanting to read your daily horoscope most people would search for their sun signs. Finding this usually does not require knowing your whole natal chart. It is easily accessible just knowing your birthdate.
